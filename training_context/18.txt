The ocean world is a fascinating environment containing countless marine species and ecosystems that contribute to the balance of the global environment. Oceans and seas cover about 70% of the Earth's surface and are home to millions of species, ranging from small fish to giant whales. Oceans contain vast resources such as oil, natural gas, and marine resources that contribute to the global economy. Coral reefs are among the most important marine ecosystems, providing shelter for many marine species. Oceans face numerous challenges, such as marine pollution, overfishing, and rising ocean temperatures due to climate change.
